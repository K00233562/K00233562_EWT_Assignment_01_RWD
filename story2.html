Charts Analysis: The Greatest Showman still rules on albums chart

By Alan Jones - March 9th 2018

The Official Charts Company Midweek Sales Flash is in.

In what is becoming an increasingly familiar tale, The Greatest Showman OST is at the top of the albums chart.

On 16,756 sales for the week so far, the album is outselling Ed Sheeran’s ÷ (4,339 sales) and Temperance Movement’s new entry A Deeper Cut (3,947 sales). Dua Lipa’s self-titled debut shoots seven places to No.4, boosted by 2,562 sales, while Camila Cabello jumps 14-5 with Camila (2,170 sales).

There’s little change in the singles chart either, with Drake still out in front with God’s Plan (18,594 sales). Rudimental’s These Days (16,885 sales), Portugal The Man’s Feel It Still (11,610 sales), Dua Lipa’s IDGAF (11,555 sales) and Keala Settle’s This Is Me (10,974 sales) complete the Top 5.
