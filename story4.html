PICNIC TAKES HOME BEST IRISH FESTIVAL AT THE IMRO AWARDS

February 22nd 2018

We were thrilled to take home the accolade for Best Irish Festival.

Decided by over 12,000 members of IMRO (Irish Music Rights Organisation), we're thrilled to announce Electric Picnic as the winner of Best Irish Festival at the IMRO Live Music Awards.

Electric Picnic 2017 saw headline slots from The xx, A Tribe Called Quest and Duran Duran, as well as outstanding performances from acts such as Rag'n'Bone Man, Giggs, Everything Everything, Saint Sister and Goat Girl to name a few.

Electric Picnic is now entering its 15th year and continues to prove itself as being more than just a music festival with the Comedy Tent providing a belly full of laughs. You'll also find an oasis of literary readings, political debate, performance poerty, science experiments, inspiring talks and out of this world Trailer Park designs.

Tickets for Electric Picnic 2018 go on sale in March.
